About Gabaldon

Gabaldon, Nueva Ecija is a peaceful municipality located at the foot of the Sierra Madre mountains in the Philippines. Known for its natural beauty, Gabaldon is home to scenic spots like Gabaldon Falls, refreshing rivers, and lush forests. It is a place rich in culture and nature, offering a calm escape from city life.
