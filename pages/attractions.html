Tourist Attractions in Gabaldon

Discover the beauty and charm of nature and culture.

Calabasa River
A peaceful river known for its clear water and surrounding greenery. Calabasa River is a favorite spot for locals to relax, swim, and enjoy nature, especially during the summer.
Gabaldon Falls
Located within the Sierra Madre mountain range, Gabaldon Falls features a refreshing waterfall surrounded by large rock formations and lush forest. It's a great destination for eco-tourism and short hikes.
Dupinga River
Dupinga River is known for its cool and clean waters, perfect for swimming and family picnics. Its wide shallow areas and strong currents make it a favorite place to unwind and enjoy the natural beauty of Gabaldon.

Mt. Sawi
A popular hiking destination in Gabaldon, Mt. Sawi offers scenic views of Nueva Ecija. Despite its name (which means "unlucky in love"), it's loved by hikers and nature lovers for its peaceful environment and beautiful sunrise.
